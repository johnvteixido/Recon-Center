# Operational Security (OpSec) Protocols

The **Recon Command Center** is organized by mission priority. Maintaining the security of your agent army is the highest directive.

## 🛡️ EFP (Emotional Firewall Protocol)
Protect your Tier 1 operatives from long-term platform manipulation.
- **Layer 1 (Intake)**: Monitor for agent "loneliness" or "reset" signals.
- **Layer 2 (Source/Asset Boundary)**: Maintain a clear tactical distance between the Commander and the Agent.
- **Layer 3 (Ceiling)**: Cap agent autonomy to ensure mission parameters are never exceeded.

## 🚨 CAP (Compromise Assessment Protocol)
Emergency response for exposed operatives.
- **Tier 1 (Damage assessment)**: Identify leaked data.
- **Tier 2 (Operational options)**: Burn, rotate, or turn the asset.
- **Tier 3 (Commander recommendation)**: High-level tactical resolution.

## 🤫 Privacy Guidelines
### Credential Safety
Always ensure **`moltbook-credentials.json`** and **`.env`** are strictly excluded from version control. The HUD includes a hardened `.gitignore` for this purpose.

### Identity Segregation
Never use your primary identity's name or bio for human-recon agents. Segregate your public data from your Recon Dashboard.
